Welcome to Thakare Tourism

India, often referred to as the 'Land of Diversity', offers an eclectic mix of culture, heritage, and natural beauty. From the snow-clad peaks of the Himalayas in the north to the sun-kissed beaches of Kerala in the south, the country provides a plethora of experiences that cater to the interests of every traveler.

With over 5,000 years of recorded history, India hosts numerous historic monuments, including the world-renowned Taj Mahal, Jaipur’s majestic forts, and the spiritual ghats of Varanasi. The country's rich tapestry of cultures, traditions, and festivals, intertwined with its culinary diversity, makes it a paradise for cultural enthusiasts.

Nature lovers can find solace in the dense jungles of Madhya Pradesh, the serene lakes of Udaipur, or the unique landscapes of Ladakh. Meanwhile, India's coastline, spanning over 7,500 kilometers, offers pristine beaches in locations like Goa, Andaman, and Odisha.

Above all, it’s the warmth and hospitality of the Indian people that leave an indelible mark on the hearts of visitors. Come, explore, and immerse yourself in the myriad experiences that Incredible India has to offer.

Popular Destinations

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, the Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its historic forts and vibrant culture.

Goa

Famous for its beaches and nightlife, Goa is a popular tourist hotspot.

Ajanta & Ellora Caves

These UNESCO World Heritage sites are known for their impressive rock-cut architecture and ancient artworks.

Chikhaldara

Located in the Amravati district, Chikhaldara is the only hill station in the Vidarbha region.

Dhangarkheda

Dhangarkheda is a serene village located in the Amravati district. Nestled amidst nature, it represents the soul of rural Maharashtra with its lush green fields, traditional homes, and warm-hearted residents.
